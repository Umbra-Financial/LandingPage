Why would I want to move my money into crypto?

Crypto currency has many options that standard finances simply lack. What makes crypto so great, is the fact that it can do all the fancy stuff that normal fiat money can do, just better and more secure.They allow you to store your value in something that you are already familiar with and aren’t subject to random market price fluctuations.

  • If you prefer the stability of the US dollar, well there are 1:1 USD backed crypto currencies called Stablecoins.

  • One benefit of having your money in crypto is higher interest rates.

  • I've seen interest rates on crypto range from 3%-10%. USD Stablecoin is usually around the higher 8% mark. Another benefit would be full ownership.

  • You don’t have to store your crypto in a bank account.
