What is Crypto?

Crypto Currency is the next step in evolving our modern day financial transactions. Currently we depend on a fiat monetary system that forces us to rely on our governments for economic stability. Cryptocurrency promises to change that dynamic by bringing economic power back to the individual.

How does it work exactly?

A good way to think about most cryptocurrency is by calling it a commodity. Just like any other commodity, It derives its value from people buying and selling it on the open market. Some crypto are worth more than others, and this is because each coin has its own unique price.

  • Just like Gold or Silver, each crypto currency has a finite amount of coins that can be generated.

  • This means that each individual coin has scarcity and balances its price through supply and demand.

  • Crypto currency has many options that standard finances simply lack. What makes crypto so great, is the fact that it can do all the fancy stuff that normal fiat money can do, just better and more secure.
